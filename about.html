ABOUT

LE SSERAFIM's fifth mini-album 'Hot' was released on March 14, 2025, serving as the third and final installment of their "Easy-Crazy-Hot" trilogy. The album marks a thematic shift where the group explores devotion and passion, choosing to "set their bodies on fire" to maintain the flame of love and resilience rather than focusing solely on fearlessness or chaos.

The project features a subdued, disco-tinged pop sound compared to their previous bombastic singles, with the title track "Hot" expressing a willingness to sacrifice everything for love. Key tracks include "Ash," a moody, atmospheric song about finding new life from pain (co-written by Huh Yunjin), and "Come Over," a retro-flavored track produced by the British band Jungle.
